Our story

About Saaz

Saaz is the annual summer cultural festival of IIT Bhilai, organized by the Cultural Council. A celebration of creativity, expression, and talent — bringing together students from across India for three days of unforgettable performances and competitions.

From classical dance to street battles, from poetry slams to band showdowns — Saaz is the stage where every art form finds its voice.

Whether you're a performer, an audience member, or a volunteer — Saaz has a place for you.
